My Story

Hey, I'm Eric.
This is my story.

This isn't just my highlight reel. It's the real story. The one with the grit and the grace. The late nights and the breakthroughs. The kind of story that reminds us we can crash hard and still rise.

Scroll
The break

I know what it feels like to break down.

Not just physically, though I know that one well. At the very foundation of my identity.

There was a season where everything looked good on paper. I was building, achieving, moving fast. But beneath it all, I was exhausted. I broke my body by pushing past every limit, seven broken vertebrae, five herniated discs. I nearly lost my marriage, not because we didn't love each other, but because I was too out of alignment to show up fully. I've buried loved ones I thought would be here forever. The grief I carried reshaped how I see the world.

The shift

And yet, from the edge of all of that, something began to shift.

I started asking better questions. I stopped pretending things were fine. I got radically honest about who I was and what I stood for. That's when the rebuild began.

Not just for my health. But my marriage. My presence. My business. My leadership. My capacity to serve and to live.

Today, my companies run without me. They're built to do real good in the world. My marriage is thriving. My body is strong. My joy is steady. But none of that came through shortcuts. It came through a path, a process, that I now offer to others.

What I believe

I help founders sit in their inner Royal.

Their King, or Queen energy. As a Royal, you're above burnout. Above codependence, above conflict. You sit in a place of deep presence and being enough.

If you're in a season of tension, transition, or rebuilding, I see you. Not because I've read the books or taken some course. Because I've lived the story.
