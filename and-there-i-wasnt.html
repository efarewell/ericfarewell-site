And There I Wasn’t.

Six years ago I hit the “pinnacle” of my success.

I finished the year with just shy of $6,000,000 of revenue in my passion based business. A business that should never have worked. When I’d launched it, the entire niche had less than 1,200 active participants in the USA.

Year after year, we’d tripled our revenue, changed our industry, and transformed lives.

Financially, I was doing better than I’d ever known.

In every other area? I was beyond lost.

Since then, I’ve found myself.

I’ve discovered my deeper purpose. I’ve built a legacy that truly matters. I’ve reclaimed my marriage, and my family. I have community and friendships I’d only dreamed existed and I own multiple companies in aviation and media, yet I don’t run them.

Most of all? I have me.

I’ve learned self control, abundance, relationships, and happiness. I’ve worked through trauma, learned to honor my inner child, and given up all my secrets. I have learned to be truly present.

18 months ago I started telling my story.

Those who listened, started asking for help.

At first I only worked 1-1 with a few founders. Over the months, I started to realize… These people were changing their lives through our work. It has been so incredibly thrilling.

I started collecting their stories, treasuring their transformations:

"Absolute game-changer for my business! In just four months, Eric helped me scale my business while cutting down my hours, giving me the freedom I desperately needed.”

"Eric helped me figure out that thriving in business and family life can happen at the same time…I’m not just building wealth, I’m building a legacy of connection and love that I didn’t know was possible."

“Since working with Eric, my income has more than doubled in 90 days. But the real transformation was how I started showing up for myself—with clarity, confidence, and alignment"

Eventually, I started longing for more.

I wanted an entire community of aligned entrepreneurs. People who do the emotional and nervous system work to be fully present so they can maximize their legacy.

So, I co-created it.

The Royals Mastermind quietly launched last month and our first cohort has begun the journey with an accelerator we’ve titled the “Kingmaker Foundry”.

I’m sharing this post to invite you, if you’re a conscious entrepreneur who’s burnt out, overloaded, and you feel like there should be more to life, I’d love to have a conversation with you.

There is no sales funnel, no pitch, but I’d love to hear your story to see if I might be able to help.

I truly felt there was no way out but to work more, to be needed by more employees, and now see how wrong I was… And I help other founders find their way to true presence.

Your Legacy Matters.

If these words resonate, they land in your inbox as they are written.
