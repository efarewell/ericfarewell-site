Meet Eric

Not just what he's built.
What he's rebuilt.

Eric Farewell is a coach, author, and entrepreneur whose life has been defined not just by what he's built, but by what he's rebuilt. He launched his first aviation business at 13 and was speaking on stages across the country before he was 20.

Then life hit hard. A back broken twice. The loss of his brother James. A marriage that nearly collapsed. Out of the wreckage came a different way of building, one rooted in presence, alignment, and soul. Today he helps founders do the same.
